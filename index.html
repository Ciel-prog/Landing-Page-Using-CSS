How FreshSense Works

Most mushroom problems start quietly. FreshSense solves this by detecting early smells that are impossible to notice without technology.

  • Step 1: Place the sensor in your grow room.
  • Step 2: The sensor analyzes air quality automatically.
  • Step 3: The app alerts you to check humidity or ventilation if risks arise.
